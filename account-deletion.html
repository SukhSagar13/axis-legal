Axis Account Deletion Request

Last updated: June 16, 2026

If you created an Axis account and want your account and associated synced data deleted, use the request path below.

Request deletion

Submit your request through this form:

Open deletion request form

What to include

  • The email address used to sign in to Axis.
  • Any identifying details needed to verify the request.
  • If relevant, the workspace or organization name connected to the account.

What gets deleted

We intend to delete the app account and associated synced workspace data tied to that account, including records such as tasks, people, distributors, retailers, orders, and synced files, unless retention is reasonably required for security, fraud prevention, abuse handling, or legal compliance.
