Axis Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Axis is a business productivity and task-management app used to track tasks, people, distributors, retailers, orders, files, and related operational data. This Privacy Policy explains what data Axis stores, how it is used, and how users can request deletion.

1. Data we collect

  • Account data such as email address and basic profile information from Google sign-in or email sign-in.
  • Business data entered by the user, including tasks, notes, people, distributors, retailers, and orders.
  • Uploaded files such as images and PDFs that the user chooses to import into the app.
  • Technical and sync metadata needed to keep the app functioning across devices.

2. How data is stored

  • Local app data may be stored on-device for offline use and faster access.
  • When cloud sync is enabled, synced records are stored in Supabase infrastructure associated with the user workspace.
  • Exported CSV, workbook, and backup files are created only when the user explicitly requests an export.

3. How data is used

  • To authenticate the user and restore their account across devices.
  • To sync user-entered records such as tasks, contacts, and business network data.
  • To let users export, share, and back up their own data.
  • To improve reliability, support troubleshooting, and maintain service integrity.

4. Service providers and sharing

Axis does not sell user data or use it for advertising. Axis uses service providers to operate core features: Supabase processes authentication, synced database records, and synced files; Google may process Google sign-in; and Google Forms and email may process support or deletion requests. These providers process data to deliver their services under their own terms and privacy commitments. Data may also leave Axis when the user explicitly exports or shares a workbook, backup, image, PDF, or other attachment through another app.

5. Security

Axis uses encrypted HTTPS connections for cloud communication, private workspace access controls in Supabase, and encrypted device storage for authentication sessions. No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed completely secure, so users should avoid entering data they are not authorized to manage.

6. User responsibility

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to upload, store, sync, and share any business, customer, employee, or third-party information they enter into Axis.

7. Data deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated synced data using the public account deletion page: Account Deletion.

8. Retention

Active account and synced workspace data is retained while needed to provide the service. Verified account deletion requests are targeted for completion within 30 calendar days. Support messages, deletion-request records, provider backups, and security logs may remain for a limited period where reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, abuse handling, backup rotation, or legal compliance.

9. Contact

For privacy or support questions, contact: axis.support.helpdesk@gmail.com
